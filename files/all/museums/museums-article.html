Traveler's Choices 2013

Top 10 Museums - United States

1. Art Institute of Chicago

Chicago, Illinois

“Beautiful art from all periods. The building itself is art. Great Impressionist collection. Just when you think you are done, there is more.”

2. Frick Collection

New York City, New York

“My Favorite of all New York Art Museums”

3. National Gallery of Art

Washington DC, District of Columbia

“Soak up the Arts”

4. Metropolitan Museum of Art

New York City, New York

“Biggest collection of Egyptian art in US”

5. Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum

Tucson, Arizona

“Great Place to see Az in a natural setting”

6. National Naval Aviation Museum

Pensacola, Florida

“This place is incredible.”

7. The National WWII Museum

New Orleans, Louisiana

“Highly recommend!! You can spend all day!”

8. Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum

Washington DC, District of Columbia

“SR-71 Blackbird at the Air and Space Museum @ Dulles Airport in Washington DC area - not downtown”

9. United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Washington DC, District of Columbia

“Dramatic, moving, respectfully done”

10. Abraham Lincoln Presidential Library and Museum

Springfield, Illinois

“It doesn't get any better than this.”
